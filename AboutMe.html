My sports interests

FOOTBALL

Football has always been important to me. I have played in teams and with friends in the street, and I still enjoy it a lot. I like both playing and watching it on TV.

Being from Bilbao and a big fan, I support Athletic Club. Experiencing matches in San Mamés is unique. The atmosphere, the supporters and the way football is lived here make it very special.

Besides my team, I also follow big competitions like the Champions League or the World Cup. I also enjoy basketball, both watching and playing with friends.
