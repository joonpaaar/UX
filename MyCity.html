Discover my city

Bilbao

Bilbao is the largest city in the Basque Country and a key economic and cultural hub in northern Spain. Once known for its industrial character, it is now famous for its urban transformation with icons like the Guggenheim Museum and the Zubizuri bridge.

It still preserves its traditional essence in places like the Old Town and Plaza Nueva. Surrounded by mountains and close to the coast, Bilbao blends modernity, tradition and nature.
